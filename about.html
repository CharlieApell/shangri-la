About Us

Shangri-La Yoga is a welcoming and inclusive community that offers a wide range of classes for practitioners of all levels, both online and in-person. Our experienced instructors guide you through your practice with compassion and expertise, helping you to deepen your mind-body connection and leave feeling refreshed, rejuvenated, and empowered.

In addition to our regular classes, we also offer private sessions, workshops, and retreats that allow you to delve deeper into your practice and connect with others on the same journey.
Our experienced instructors can tailor these offerings to your specific needs and goals, helping you to achieve your personal wellness goals.

At Shangri-La Yoga, we believe that yoga is much more than just a physical practice. It's a way of life that can help you to cultivate inner peace, balance, and mindfulness. Our community of supportive, kind, and uplifting individuals share a passion for wellness and self-care, and we invite you to join us on this journey.
